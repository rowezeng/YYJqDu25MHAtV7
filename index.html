About the teacher

Ms. Zeng Si Ning is a foreign language teacher with decades of experience. Before coming to the United States, she was an associate professor at Beijing Normal University. She taught at Irvine Chinese School from 2016-2018 and is currently the head of Chinese Storytime at the Arcadia Public Library.

曾思凝来美前是北京师范大学汉语学院的副教授，来美后在尔湾中文学校任教，成功完成中文启蒙教学，中文阅读与写作以及中文书法等多种教学。目前是阿卡迪亚中文故事会的主要发起人和领导者.
Class schedule

Each course will have 2 lessons a week, for a total of 80 lessons taught from August 9th to May 22nd. Each lesson will last 90 minutes.

课程表每门课每周上两次，每次课时90分钟。从八月九号到明年五月二十二号总共上80节课。
