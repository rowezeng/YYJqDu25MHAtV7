About the teacher

Ms. Zeng Si Ning is a foreign language teacher with decades of experience.

Before coming to the United States, she was an associate professor at Beijing Normal University.

She taught at Irvine Chinese School from 2017-2018 and is currently the head of Chinese Storytime at the Arcadia Public Library.

曾思凝来美前是北京师范大学汉语学院的副教授，来美后在尔湾中文学校任教，成功完成中文启蒙教学，中文阅读与写作以及中文书法等多种教学。目前是阿卡迪亚中文故事会的主要发起人和领导者.
